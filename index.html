The Work of Art in the Aage of Mechanical Reproduction

The Work of Art in the Age of Mechanical Reproduction focuses on how the mechanization of art- i.e. the ability to reproduce it via photography and film- removes the "aura" from the art. This allows for art to be used as a commodity, another byproduct of capitalism in the western world.
